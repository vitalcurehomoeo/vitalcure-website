🌿 FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about homoeopathic treatment at VitalCure.

What is Homoeopathy and how does it work? ▾
Homoeopathy is a natural system of medicine based on the principle of "like cures like." It uses highly diluted remedies to stimulate the body's own healing mechanism. Treatment focuses on the individual as a whole, considering physical, mental, and emotional aspects.
Is Homoeopathy safe for all age groups? ▾
Yes, homoeopathy is gentle and safe for people of all ages, including infants, pregnant women, and the elderly. Medicines are non-toxic and suitable for long-term use under proper guidance.
How long does treatment take to show results? ▾
The time required depends on the condition. Acute illnesses often respond quickly, while chronic conditions may take longer as the treatment works on the root cause for lasting relief.
Are there any side effects of homoeopathic medicines? ▾
Homoeopathic medicines are natural and generally free from side effects when prescribed correctly. They are designed to work gently without causing harm to the body.
Can I take Homoeopathy along with other medications? ▾
Yes, homoeopathy can be taken alongside conventional treatments. It may support overall healing and, in some cases, help reduce dependency on other medicines over time (with medical supervision).
Is Homoeopathy effective for chronic diseases? ▾
Yes, it is especially effective in managing chronic conditions such as allergies, skin disorders, migraines, arthritis, and hormonal imbalances by addressing the underlying cause rather than just symptoms.
Are there any diet restrictions during treatment? ▾
Only minimal precautions are usually advised, such as avoiding strong flavors like coffee or mint close to medicine intake. Otherwise, patients can maintain a normal diet.
Is online / tele-consultation available? ▾
Yes, we provide online consultation at VitalCure. You just need to call to book your online consultation slot.
Can pregnant women take Homoeopathy? ▾
Yes, homoeopathy is generally safe during pregnancy when taken under professional supervision and can help manage common issues like nausea, acidity, and back pain.
What happens during the first consultation? ▾
The first consultation involves a detailed discussion about your symptoms, medical history, lifestyle, and emotional health. This helps in selecting the most suitable remedy for you.
